Our Story

A Family Table at the Lake

Clementine's Maison is a locally family-owned and operated wood-fire steakhouse in the heart of Osage Beach. We pair prime cuts and fresh seafood with the kind of hospitality only a family can offer.

Every plate leaves our open-flame hearth with care — from dry-aged ribeye to butter-basted Gulf prawns. Come for dinner; stay for the evening.
