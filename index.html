Our Story

A Heritage of Hospitality

Three generations. Three coasts. One table.

Clementine's began in Columbia, Missouri at Churchill's, the fine-dining room where Brad and Jennifer Weiss learned the craft. It traveled south to the Florida Keys, where the family opened Broil Small Island Steakhouse and refined a love affair with wood-fire and seafood. Now, with Manning and Lola at their side, the Weiss family has come home to the Lake — and named the maison after Clementine Churchill, Winston's wife, as a tribute to where it all began.

Meet the Chef

Caroline Kaiser

Johnson & Wales. Columbia, Missouri. Home.

Trained in classical French technique and steeped in years as a sous chef in some of the Midwest's most respected kitchens, Chef Caroline is a Columbia native and a longtime friend of the Weiss family. Joining Clementine's wasn't a career move — it was a homecoming.

"Cooking for the people you love. There's no better seat at the table."
